1. Introduction

Play Lounge ("Play Lounge", "we", "us", or "our") is operated by NeuEra Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our multiplayer board game platform, available as a web application at playlounge.live and as a native Android application (package ID: live.playlounge.android).

Play Lounge is a free multiplayer gaming platform supported by advertising. We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed our service to collect virtually no personal information. You can enjoy our games completely anonymously without creating an account.

PRIVACY-FIRST DESIGN: Play Lounge operates on a session-based model with no user accounts, no signup required, and no personal information collected. All your data is temporary and deleted when you close the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our multiplayer gaming service. Here's exactly what we collect and don't collect:

What We Collect Directly:

  • Random Player ID: A temporary, randomly generated identifier created when you start a game session. This ID is NOT linked to any personal information and is deleted when you close the app.
  • Session Token (JWT): A temporary authentication token with 24-hour expiration, used only for reconnecting to your game if you briefly lose connection. Deleted when you close the app.
  • Room Codes: 6-character codes for private games with friends. These codes expire after 5 minutes of inactivity and are not stored permanently.
  • Game State Data: Your current game progress (card positions, scores, etc.) stored temporarily in your browser's sessionStorage/localStorage. This is deleted when you close the app or browser.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identification
  • User accounts or login credentials (no signup required)
  • Location data (beyond general country/city for ads - see Advertising section)
  • Device identifiers, IMEI, or MAC addresses
  • Contact lists or phone numbers
  • Photos, videos, or camera access
  • Microphone or audio recordings
  • Browsing history or activity outside Play Lounge
  • Payment information (we don't sell anything)
  • Chat or messaging data (we have no chat features)

Technical Information:

  • Connection Data: Your IP address is temporarily used to establish WebSocket connections to our game server (hosted on Fly.io). We do not log or store IP addresses long-term.
  • Browser Storage: We use sessionStorage and localStorage (browser features) to store game state for reconnection purposes only. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you close the browser/app.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Data Collection

Play Lounge is a free service supported by advertisements. While we do not collect or access advertising data ourselves, our advertising partners collect certain information independently to display and measure ads.

What Advertising Networks May Collect:

Our ad partners (Google AdMob and Google AdSense) may collect:

  • Device Advertising ID: Google Advertising ID (GAID) on Android devices, used for personalized ads
  • IP Address: For general location (country/city level, NOT precise GPS location)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, screen size
  • App Usage Patterns: How often you use Play Lounge, session duration (for ad frequency)
  • Ad Interaction: Which ads you view or click on

Important Distinctions:

  • WE DO NOT COLLECT THIS DATA: Advertising data goes directly to ad networks; we cannot access it
  • WE DO NOT SELL YOUR DATA: We don't collect personal data, so there's nothing to sell
  • AD NETWORKS OPERATE INDEPENDENTLY: They have their own privacy policies (see links below)

Advertising Network Privacy Policies:

  • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Google Ads Privacy
  • How Google Uses Data: Google Partner Sites

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the minimal information we collect for these purposes only:

  • Provide Gaming Service: Enable real-time multiplayer gameplay through WebSocket connections
  • Session Management: Maintain your game state and allow reconnection if you lose connection briefly
  • Matchmaking: Connect players for random matchmaking (anonymously, no profiles created)
  • Private Rooms: Enable private games with friends using room codes
  • Cross-Platform Play: Allow web and mobile players to play together seamlessly
  • Service Improvement: Identify and fix bugs, optimize performance
  • Security: Prevent abuse, cheating, and denial-of-service attacks

WE DO NOT: Track your behavior, build user profiles, or use your data for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage & Retention

All data collected by Play Lounge is ephemeral (temporary):

Session-Only Storage:

  • Random Player IDs: Deleted when you close the app or browser
  • Session Tokens: Expire after 24 hours; deleted when app is closed
  • Room Codes: Expire after 5 minutes of inactivity
  • Game State: Stored in browser sessionStorage/localStorage, cleared when session ends
  • WebSocket Connections: Terminated immediately when you disconnect

Server-Side Storage:

  • Active Game Rooms: Stored temporarily in server memory during gameplay, deleted when game ends
  • Server Logs: Minimal connection logs retained for up to 7 days for debugging and security purposes only

Browser Storage:

  • sessionStorage: Game state for active sessions, cleared when browser tab is closed
  • localStorage: Used only for reconnection to interrupted games, cleared when you close the app
  • Cookies: We do NOT use cookies for our services (ad networks may use cookies independently)

NO PERMANENT STORAGE: We do not maintain databases of user information, game history, or personal data. Everything is session-based and temporary.

6. Information Sharing & Third Parties

Because we collect virtually no personal information, there's almost nothing to share. Here's who we work with:

Third-Party Services:

  • Fly.io (Server Hosting): Our game server is hosted on Fly.io infrastructure. They may process connection data (IP addresses) as part of providing hosting services. See Fly.io Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob (Advertising): Displays ads in mobile app. Collects advertising data independently (see Section 3).
  • Google AdSense (Advertising): Displays ads on web platform. Collects advertising data independently (see Section 3).

We Do NOT Share Data With:

  • Analytics services (we don't use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or similar)
  • Marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes

Legal Disclosure:

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • Compliance with legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protection of our rights and safety
  • Prevention of illegal activities or fraud

However: Because we collect no personal information or account data, there is minimal information we could provide even if legally required.

7. Children's Privacy

Play Lounge is designed to be safe for all ages, including children. Our app is rated PEGI 3 / Everyone and complies with children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Why Play Lounge is Safe for Children:

  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, locations, or any identifying information from anyone, including children
  • No User Accounts: No signup or registration required, so no account data to protect
  • No Chat or Social Features: No text chat, voice chat, or user-generated content that could expose children to inappropriate interactions
  • Anonymous Play: All players are anonymous; you can't identify who you're playing with
  • Family-Friendly Ads: Advertising networks are configured for family-friendly, age-appropriate ads
  • No In-App Purchases: No payment features that children could access

Advertising & Children:

Our advertising partners (AdMob and AdSense) are required to comply with children's advertising regulations:

  • Ads are filtered for age-appropriateness
  • Personalized ads are limited for child-directed content
  • Ad networks must comply with COPPA, GDPR-K, and other children's privacy laws

Parental Rights:

Parents/guardians have the right to:

  • Review what information we collect (answer: virtually none)
  • Request deletion of any data (though we store no personal data)
  • Refuse further collection (by uninstalling the app)

Contact us at hello@neuera.app with any concerns about children's privacy.

8. Data Security

Although we collect minimal data, we still implement strong security measures:

  • Encrypted Connections: All communication between your device and our server uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • WebSocket Security: Secure WebSocket (WSS) connections for real-time gameplay
  • No Persistent Storage: Session-only data reduces risk of data breaches
  • Server Security: Fly.io infrastructure provides DDoS protection and secure hosting
  • Token Expiration: Session tokens automatically expire after 24 hours
  • Room Code Expiration: Temporary room codes expire after 5 minutes
  • No Central Database: We don't maintain databases of user information, eliminating database breach risks

BEST SECURITY IS NO DATA: Our security strategy is simple - we don't collect or store personal information, so there's nothing to lose in a data breach.

9. Your Choices & Controls

You have several options to control your privacy:

Advertising Controls:

  • Opt Out of Personalized Ads:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • Web: Visit Google Ad Settings
  • Reset Advertising ID:
    • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • Limit Ad Tracking: Available in device privacy settings

Data Deletion:

  • Automatic: All session data is automatically deleted when you close the app
  • Manual: Clear your browser's localStorage/sessionStorage in browser settings
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Cookies:

We don't use cookies ourselves, but ad networks may use cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may affect ad display)
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set cookies to expire when browser closes

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have legal privacy rights. Because we collect virtually no personal information, most of these rights don't apply in practice, but we respect them nonetheless:

General Rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what data we have (answer: none, except temporary session data)
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (automatically happens when you close the app)
  • Right to Portability: Download your data (not applicable - no stored personal data)
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing (you can stop using the service anytime)
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data collection (close the app or uninstall)

Regional Privacy Laws:

  • EU Residents (GDPR): Rights to access, deletion, portability, restriction, objection
  • California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): Rights to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • UK Residents (UK GDPR): Same rights as EU GDPR
  • Canadian Residents (PIPEDA): Rights to access and correction
  • Brazilian Residents (LGPD): Rights to access, correction, deletion

Exercising Your Rights:

To exercise any privacy rights, contact us at hello@neuera.app. We will respond within 30 days. However, please note that because we collect no personal information or user accounts, most requests will receive the response: "No personal data stored."
