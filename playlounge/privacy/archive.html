Current Version
October 29, 2025
Changes in this version:

Initial Privacy Policy Release - This is the first version of our privacy policy, establishing our privacy-first approach to multiplayer gaming with minimal data collection.

Key Features:
  • Established no-accounts model (anonymous play without signup)
  • Session-only data collection with automatic deletion
  • Clear separation of our data practices vs. advertising networks
  • Family-friendly design safe for all ages (PEGI 3 / Everyone rated)
  • COPPA compliance for children's privacy
  • Transparent disclosure of AdMob and AdSense advertising data collection
  • No tracking, analytics, or permanent storage of user data
  • Fly.io infrastructure for game hosting with minimal data retention
  • WebSocket-based real-time gameplay with session tokens (24-hour expiry)
  • Comprehensive user rights under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws

Future Versions

Future updates to our privacy policy will be listed here. We are committed to transparency and will document all changes with clear explanations of what has been modified and why.

Our Update Process:
  1. Review: We regularly review our privacy practices and legal requirements
  2. Update: When changes are needed, we update the policy with a new version
  3. Notification: Users are notified through website updates and in-app notifications

  5. Continued Use: Continued use of Play Lounge after updates constitutes acceptance

About This Archive

Transparency Commitment

We believe in complete transparency regarding our privacy practices. This archive ensures that users can always see what our privacy policy contained at any point in time.

Legal Compliance

Maintaining version history helps us comply with various privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, COPPA) that require transparency about data practices and policy changes.

User Trust

By maintaining this archive, we demonstrate our commitment to user trust and accountability in our privacy practices, especially important for family-friendly apps.

Privacy-First Design

Our privacy-first approach means we collect virtually no personal data. This archive documents our ongoing commitment to minimal data collection and session-only storage.
