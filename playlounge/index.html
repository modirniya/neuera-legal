About Play Lounge

Service Overview

Play Lounge offers real-time multiplayer board games in your browser or on Android. Play with friends using room codes, or match with random players anonymously. No accounts or personal information required.

Platform Availability

  • Web Browser (playlounge.live)
  • Android App (via Google Play Store)
  • Cross-platform compatibility

Privacy Features

  • Anonymous play - no signup needed
  • Session-based data (auto-deleted)
  • No chat or social features
  • Family-friendly (safe for children)

Technical Details

  • WebSocket-based real-time gameplay
  • Hosted on Fly.io infrastructure
  • Session tokens expire after 24 hours
  • Room codes expire after 5 minutes

Pricing

Free service supported by advertisements (Google AdMob and AdSense). No in-app purchases. No premium features.

Support

For technical support, privacy questions, or general inquiries about Play Lounge, contact us at hello@neuera.app

Our Privacy-First Commitment

No Personal Data

We don't collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any identifying information. Play completely anonymously.

Session-Only Storage

All game data is temporary and deleted when you close the app. We don't maintain databases of user information.

No Tracking

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking services. Your privacy is respected.

Transparent Advertising

Our privacy policy clearly explains what ad networks collect (separately from us) and how to opt out.
