2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

2.1 What We Provide

Play Lounge is a free, ad-supported multiplayer board game platform that allows users to:

  • Play classic board games in real-time with other players
  • Create private game rooms using 6-character room codes
  • Join random matchmaking for anonymous play
  • Play cross-platform (web and Android devices)

2.2 No Account Required

Play Lounge operates on a session-based model:

  • No registration, signup, or user account required
  • All gameplay is anonymous
  • Random player IDs generated per session
  • No personal information collected
  • Session data deleted when you close the app

2.3 Ad-Supported Model

Play Lounge is completely free to use, supported by advertisements from Google AdMob (mobile) and Google AdSense (web). Ads may be displayed:

Play Lounge offers various board games. Game availability may change without notice as we add or remove games from the platform.

3.1 Session-Based Access

  • Sessions created automatically when you join or start a game
