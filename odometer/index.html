About Odo

App Overview

Odo is a minimalist mileage tracking app for iOS and Android that lets you manually log trips and expenses with optional location snapshots for geographic context. Perfect for business mileage deductions, expense reimbursements, or personal vehicle tracking.

Core Features

  • Manual trip logging with odometer readings
  • Optional location snapshots (single GPS capture per entry)
  • Expense tracking (fuel, tolls, parking, maintenance)
  • Business vs. personal categorization
  • IRS mileage rate calculations
  • Multiple vehicle support
  • Monthly statistics and summaries
  • CSV import/export with location data
  • PDF report generation

Privacy Features

  • All data stored locally in SQLite database
  • Optional location snapshots - NOT continuous tracking
  • Location permission is optional - app works without it
  • No user accounts required
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No cloud sync - data stays on your device

Platform

  • iOS and Android (Flutter)
  • Local-only data storage
  • No cloud sync or backup
  • User-controlled exports

Pricing

Odo is completely free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no advertisements. All features are available to all users.

Our Privacy-First Commitment

Zero Collection

We don't collect any data. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports. Your data never leaves your device.

Local Storage Only

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. No cloud sync, no remote storage.

Optional Location Snapshots

Location capture is optional and one-time per entry. We don't track your movements - just a single GPS point for context if you choose.

Your Data, Your Device

All data stays in a local database on your device. Export anytime to CSV or PDF. Uninstall to delete everything.
