About Odometer

App Overview

Odometer is a minimalist mileage tracking app for Android that lets you manually log trips and expenses without any GPS tracking. Perfect for business mileage deductions, expense reimbursements, or personal vehicle tracking.

Core Features

  • Manual trip logging with odometer readings
  • Expense tracking (fuel, tolls, parking, maintenance)
  • Business vs. personal categorization
  • IRS mileage rate calculations
  • Multiple vehicle support
  • Monthly statistics and summaries
  • CSV import/export
  • PDF report generation

Privacy Features

  • 100% offline - no internet permission
  • No GPS or location tracking
  • No user accounts required
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No third-party services
  • All data in local SQLite database

Platform

  • Android (Flutter)
  • Completely offline operation
  • No cloud sync or backup
  • User-controlled exports

Pricing

Odometer is completely free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no advertisements. All features are available to all users.

Our Privacy-First Commitment

Zero Collection

We don't collect any data. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports. The app has no ability to send data anywhere.

100% Offline

Odometer has no internet permission. It physically cannot connect to any servers or transmit any information.

No GPS Tracking

Unlike other mileage apps, we don't track your location. All mileage is calculated from odometer readings you manually enter.

Your Data, Your Device

All data stays in a local database on your device. Export anytime to CSV or PDF. Uninstall to delete everything.
