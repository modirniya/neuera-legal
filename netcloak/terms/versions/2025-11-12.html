2. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

2.1 What We Provide

NetCloak is a free, ad-supported VPN service using the WireGuard protocol. The Service provides encrypted internet connections through our server infrastructure.

2.2 Service Tier

The Service offers a single free tier with data quota limits:

  • Free Tier: 20 Mbps download/upload, 24-hour sessions, 1.05GB data limit per session
  • Data Extension: Watch advertisements to add 500MB data quota to your session
  • Grace Period: 5 minutes of continued access after data limit is exceeded
  • Warning System: Proactive alerts at 15% (Low) and 5% (Critical) remaining data/time

2.3 Ad-Supported Model

Access to the Service requires viewing video advertisements (minimum 8 seconds) through Google AdMob. This is how we keep the Service free.

2.4 Server Infrastructure

  • Service availability depends on server capacity
